The Web Browser: The Essential Client

The World Wide Web is an interconnected and humungous system that has reshaped the modern life we live in today, and at its core lies a fundamental piece of software which is the web browser. While it may seem like a simple connection to the internet, the browser is actually a crucial and complex piece of technology that serves at the main interface between the web and the user. It is the client within the client/server architecture of the web, responsible for interpreting, presenting and requesting web content.
